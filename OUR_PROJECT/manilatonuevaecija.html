Basic Information

Manila to Nueva Ecija is approximately a 3 to 4-hour drive, covering around 120 to 160 kilometers depending on the destination within the province. The route typically goes through the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and passes through towns like San Fernando and Gapan. Nueva Ecija is known as the "Rice Granary of the Philippines" due to its vast rice fields and agricultural landscape. Popular spots include the Minalungao National Park and the historic town of Cabanatuan, which played a role in World War II.
