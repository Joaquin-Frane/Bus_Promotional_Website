Basic Information

Manila to Sorsogon is approximately a 10 to 12-hour drive, covering around 600 kilometers via the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and the Pan-Philippine Highway. Sorsogon, located at the southern tip of Luzon, is known for its beautiful beaches, scenic landscapes, and rich marine biodiversity, making it a popular destination for eco-tourism. The province is home to the famed whale shark sightings in Donsol, where visitors can swim and interact with these gentle giants in their natural habitat. Additionally, Sorsogon features historical sites, hot springs, and cultural attractions, providing a diverse experience for travelers.

Trivia

The journey from Manila to Sorsogon takes you through stunning landscapes, including mountainous regions and picturesque coastal views, showcasing the beauty of Southern Luzon. Sorsogon is renowned for Donsol, often called the "Whale Shark Capital of the World," where visitors can swim with gentle whale sharks, known locally as "butanding." The province is also home to beautiful natural attractions such as the Bulusan Lake and the scenic beaches of Matnog, known for their pristine white sand. Additionally, Sorsogon has a rich cultural heritage, with historical sites like the Sorsogon Cathedral and the old Spanish-era watchtowers dotting the coastline.
