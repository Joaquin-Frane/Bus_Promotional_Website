Basic Information

Manila to Bolinao is approximately a 5 to 6-hour drive, covering around 250 kilometers via the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and the MacArthur Highway. Bolinao, located in Pangasinan, is famous for its beautiful beaches, including Patar Beach, known for its white sand and stunning sunsets. The area is also home to attractions like the Bolinao Falls, Cape Bolinao Lighthouse, and various historical sites. With its vibrant marine life and natural beauty, Bolinao is a popular destination for beach lovers, adventure seekers, and nature enthusiasts.

Trivia

The journey from Manila to Bolinao takes you through the scenic landscapes of Northern Luzon, showcasing rice fields, mountains, and coastal views. Bolinao is renowned for its stunning beaches, especially Patar Beach, which has been featured in various travel publications for its pristine beauty. The Cape Bolinao Lighthouse, built in 1905, is one of the oldest lighthouses in the Philippines and offers panoramic views of the coastline. Additionally, Bolinao is home to the Bolinao Falls, a popular spot for swimming and enjoying nature, making it a hidden gem for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
