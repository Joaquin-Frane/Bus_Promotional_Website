Basic Information

Manila to Quezon Province is approximately 130 kilometers southeast of the capital, making it a popular destination for weekend getaways. The journey typically takes about 3 to 4 hours by car or bus, primarily via the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and the Maharlika Highway. Quezon is known for its beautiful beaches, lush mountains, and vibrant festivals, such as the Pahiyas Festival, which showcases colorful decorations made from rice and local produce. The province is also rich in cultural heritage, with historical sites and delicious local delicacies, including the famous Lucban longganisa and espasol.
