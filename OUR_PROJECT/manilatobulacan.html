Basic Information

Manila to Bulacan is a relatively short journey, approximately 35 to 50 kilometers away, depending on the specific destination within Bulacan. The travel time by car or bus typically ranges from 1 to 2 hours, making it a convenient trip for day trips or weekend getaways. Bulacan is rich in history and culture, known for its significant role in the Philippine revolution and for being the birthplace of notable figures like Emilio Aguinaldo and Apolinario Mabini.

Trivia

Bulacan is also known for its traditional crafts, particularly in the production of high-quality slippers and wood carvings. Additionally, the province hosts the annual Singkaban Festival, celebrating its rich cultural heritage through various activities, parades, and exhibits.
