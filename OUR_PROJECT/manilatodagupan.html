Basic Information

Manila to Dagupan is approximately a 4 to 5-hour drive, covering around 210 kilometers via the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway (TPLEX). Dagupan, located in Pangasinan, is known as the "Bangus Capital of the Philippines" due to its famous milkfish (bangus) industry. The city is a vibrant urban center with a rich history, including its role as a commercial hub during the Spanish period. Visitors often enjoy its scenic coastal areas and local delicacies, especially the renowned Dagupan bangus.

Trivia
